# 📊 E-Commerce Data Analysis & Insights

An end-to-end data analysis project using Python to uncover actionable business insights from transactional e-commerce data. This project covers data cleaning, preprocessing, and exploratory data analysis (EDA) to understand sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance.

---

## 🚀 Key Business Insights & Visualizations

### 1. Monthly Sales Trend (Time-Series Analysis)
We tracked the business revenue over a 12-month period to observe growth and seasonal shifts. 

* **Key Finding:** A massive spike in sales was detected in **November 2011**, driven by holiday shopping traffic.

![Monthly Sales Trend](monthly_sales_trend.png)

---

### 2. Geographic Revenue: Top 5 Countries
By aggregating total sales across different countries, we identified the primary revenue-generating markets.

* **Key Finding:** The **United Kingdom** is the leading market for this business, significantly outperforming other countries.

### 3. Product Demand: Top 5 Best-Sellers
Analyzed order descriptions and quantities to find out which items customers purchase the most, aiding in stock and inventory management.

* **Key Finding:** Products like *Paper Craft, Little Birdie* and *Medium Ceramic Top Storage Jar* recorded the highest sales volumes.

### 4. Peak Shopping Hours
By grouping transactions by the hour of the day, we pinpointed exactly when customers are most active.

* **Key Finding:** Order volume peaks around **12:00 PM (Midday)**, making it the ideal time for marketing campaigns.
